to be me is to be you

who am i?

Among the wastelands, past the seas and

The jungles. The mountains and the desert,

I walk, I run, I swim to you.

With wings, spread out with a ray of feathers,

I soar. Flying above, gliding through the trees and the grey buildings,

And you fly beside me, within me.

To erase you means to pick apart each blood vessel,

Each atom that makes me whole, each bone that protrudes out my body.

For, to eradicate you, I must destroy myself by proxy.

Like a blade of grass, I shall stand tall, as your loyal follower and face

your foes and enemies. Don't be afraid, I am right here with you You whisper to me, and I nod.

For, you are me, I am you.
